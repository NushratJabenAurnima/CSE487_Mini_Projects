- Analyze policy practices related to **privacy, user rights, fairness, and accountability**

## üß© Analysis Areas & Key Findings (from Poster)

### ‚úÖ Privacy Compliance
- foodpanda collects **personal, location, and transaction data**
- Data is used for **orders, delivery, and account management**
- Users can **access, correct, and delete** data
- **Data retention** and **cross-border safeguards** are not clearly explained

### ‚úÖ User Rights & Responsibilities
- Users must accept Terms & Conditions to use the platform
- **Data deletion timelines** are not clearly defined

### ‚úÖ Prohibited Activities
- Fraudulent, illegal, or abusive activities are prohibited
- Accounts may be suspended for violations
- Enforcement is discretionary with **limited user remedy**

### ‚úÖ Intellectual Property Rights
- foodpanda‚Äôs trademarks and platform content are protected
- Reuse of platform materials is restricted
- IP control and liability appear **platform-favored**

### ‚úÖ Termination & Account Suspension
- Accounts may be suspended/terminated for policy violations
- Actions may occur **without prior notice**
- **No clear suspension reasons or appeal process** are stated

### ‚úÖ Transparency & Accessibility
- Terms are available via website/app and apply upon continued use
- Users are expected to review updates independently
- Terms are **lengthy and not user-friendly**
- Key changes are **not clearly highlighted**

### ‚úÖ Updates & Notifications
- Terms may be updated at any time
- Continued use means acceptance of changes
- Users must check updates themselves
- No guaranteed user notification or clear update history

### ‚úÖ Applicable Law
- Governed by local laws of the relevant foodpanda entity
- Disputes are handled by local courts
- No user choice or alternative dispute resolution, which may disadvantage cross-border users

### ‚úÖ Comparison with Industry Standards
- Follows local laws and country-specific terms
- Privacy practices resemble industry norms, but **do not guarantee GDPR-level compliance**
- User protections and data rights vary by country with limited transparency

## üõ°Ô∏è Recommendations (Poster Summary)

- Review ToS for legal compliance
- Strengthen data protection alignment with GDPR/CCPA-style expectations and local laws
- Use fair dispute handling approaches such as **mediation or arbitration**
- Improve transparency through shorter, clearer policy sections and visible change summaries
- Add clear timelines for data retention and deletion and clarify cross-border protections
- Provide a clearer account suspension reason + appeal process
